About me

My name is Sergei Sekhleian and I am a UX/UI designer based in Mijas, Spain. I was born in 1990 and have been working in the UX/UI field since 2011. I specialize in creating user-friendly interfaces that are both visually appealing and effective. My design process begins with understanding the user's needs and goals, then creating a design that meets those needs while also being aesthetically pleasing.


I have a passion for creating innovative designs that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. My portfolio showcases some of my work, which includes projects for websites, mobile applications, and web-based applications. I strive to create user experiences that are intuitive and engaging for users of all levels.


I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow as a designer. If you would like to learn more about me and my work, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
